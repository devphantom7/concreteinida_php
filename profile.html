Building Stronger Foundations, Together

Delivering Premium-Grade Ready Mix Concrete and Bitumen Solutions for Over a Decade

Founded in 2005 by visionary leaders Mr. Dhanbir Suri and Mr. Asif Patel, Concretech India has become a trusted name in the construction industry, specializing in high-quality Ready Mix Concrete (RMC) and Bitumen solutions. With our headquarters in Mumbai and plants strategically located for optimal delivery across Maharashtra, we provide custom-mixed concrete that meets global standards for durability and performance.

Our state-of-the-art facilities, including fully automated batching plants, ensure precision in every batch, while our expert team ensures timely delivery to project sites. Concretech India is proud to uphold rigorous quality standards (ISO 9001:2000, MCGM, QCI) and is continually improving its processes to stay ahead in a competitive industry.
