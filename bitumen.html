What is Bitumen?

Bitumen is a natural binding agent derived from petroleum, valued for its waterproof and adhesive properties. As a key component in asphalt, it helps create stable, durable road surfaces and provides essential waterproofing in construction applications. Bitumen’s versatility and resilience make it indispensable across construction projects.
